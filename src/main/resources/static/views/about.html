Welcome to Search of the Fallen, a reading companion for 'Malazan: Book of the Fallen'. MBotF is one of the most difficult and challenging series I've undertaken as a reader. During my first read through I have often found myself lost, unable to remember a character or event, and hunting through the pages to try and find the last reference. After a while this grew tiresome, so I created this application to expedite the process. It has been helpful to me so far, and I hope it will be helpful to other readers as well.

If have a bug to report or a feature to request please contact me here. KEEP IN MIND I am still reading the books through for the first time, so please don't drop any spoilers on me.

If you don't find the answer to your question here try the official subreddit or the Malazan Wiki, both of which have been massively helpful during my read through.

Hosting this site on AWS, with low traffic, the average monthly bill has been about $25. I don't host any ads, or make any money from this site.
